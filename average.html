Average Workout Plan (Men 18-24% / Women 31-36% BF)

Goal

Fat Loss / Building Foundational Strength

Target Audience

Men (18–24% BF) / Women (31–36% BF)

Training Level

Beginner to Intermediate

Caloric Intake

Moderate deficit (300–500 kcal/day)

Key Focus

Consistency, compound movements, moderate cardio, improving body composition

Weekly Workout Split (5 Days + 1 Optional Active Recovery Day)

Day 1 – Upper Body Strength (Push + Pull)

  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3×10
  • Seated Shoulder Press – 3×12
  • Assisted Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown – 3×10
  • One-arm Dumbbell Row – 3×12 each side
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension – 3×12
  • Dumbbell Hammer Curls – 3×12

Rest: 60–90 sec

Day 2 – Lower Body + Core

  • Goblet Squats – 4×12
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3×10
  • Step-ups or Lunges – 3×10/leg
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3×15
  • Leg Raises – 3×15
  • Plank – 3×30–60 sec

Rest: 60 sec

Day 3 – Cardio Intervals + Core

  • 25 min HIIT:
    • 30 sec sprint / 90 sec walk × 6–8 rounds (treadmill, bike, rower)
  • Russian Twists – 3×30 sec
  • Bicycle Crunches – 3×15
  • Hanging Knee Raises – 3×12
  • Foam Rolling – 5–10 min cool down

Day 4 – Total Body Functional Circuit

Circuit (Repeat 3–4 Rounds)

  • Kettlebell Swings – 15
  • Push-ups – 10–15
  • Air Squats – 15
  • Dumbbell Thrusters – 10
  • Jumping Jacks – 30 sec

Rest 60–90 sec between rounds

Focus on form and tempo. Don’t rush!

Day 5 – Full Body Strength

  • Deadlifts – 3×8
  • Barbell or Dumbbell Rows – 3×10
  • Incline Bench Press – 3×8
  • Hip Thrusts – 3×10
  • Front Raises (light dumbbells) – 3×12
  • Cable Woodchoppers – 3×12/side

Rest: 90 sec

Day 6 – Optional Active Recovery

  • Brisk walk or light jog – 30 min
  • Dynamic stretching or yoga – 20 min
  • Focus on mobility, breathwork, and recovery

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Calories: 300–500 kcal deficit/day
  • Protein: 2g/kg (for muscle preservation)
  • Carbs: Moderate (1.5–3g/kg), around workouts
  • Fats: Moderate (0.8–1g/kg)

Hydration: 3–4L/day + electrolytes

Keep meals whole-food based. Prioritize veggies, lean proteins, healthy fats, and whole grains. Minimize processed carbs and sugar.

Supplement Suggestions (Optional)

  • Whey or plant protein – hit daily protein target
  • Multivitamin – general nutrient support
  • Creatine – strength & fat-free mass retention
  • Green tea or L-carnitine – gentle fat metabolism support (optional)
  • Electrolytes – especially during high cardio phases
