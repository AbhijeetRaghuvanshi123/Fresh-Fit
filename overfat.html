Overfat Workout Plan (Men 25%+ / Women 37%+ BF)

Goal

Fat Loss First, Strength Second

Target Audience

Men (25%+ BF) / Women (37%+ BF)

Training Level

Beginner or returning after long break

Caloric Intake

Moderate deficit (500–700 kcal/day)

Key Focus

Sustainable fat loss, cardiovascular health, foundational movement, mobility

Weekly Workout Plan (4–5 Days Training + 1–2 Active Recovery)

Day 1 – Full Body Strength (Machines + Dumbbells)

  • Leg Press – 3×12
  • Dumbbell Chest Press – 3×10
  • Seated Row (machine or cable) – 3×10
  • Bodyweight Box Squats – 3×12
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 2×12
  • Standing Cable Woodchoppers – 2×15

Rest: 90 sec between sets

Goal: Movement confidence, range of motion

Day 2 – Low Impact Cardio + Core

  • 30–40 min brisk walk, incline treadmill, or cycling
  • Core Circuit ×3 rounds:
    • Bird-Dogs – 10/side
    • Dead Bug – 10/side
    • Seated Knee Raises – 15
    • Plank (on elbows) – 30 sec

Day 3 – Strength + Mobility Focus

  • Goblet Squats – 3×10
  • Assisted Pull-downs – 3×12
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3×10
  • Hip Bridges – 3×15
  • Dumbbell Curls – 3×12
  • Standing Overhead Press (light DBs) – 2×12
  • Cat/Cow Stretch – 2×10
  • World’s Greatest Stretch – 2 reps/side

Rest: 60–90 sec

Day 4 – Rest or Active Recovery

  • 30 min slow walk or swimming
  • Optional: light yoga / foam rolling / stretching

Day 5 – Circuit Training (Light Weights + Cardio Blend)

Repeat 3–4 Rounds:

  • Step-ups or March in Place – 1 min
  • Seated Shoulder Press – 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts – 12 reps
  • Stationary Bike or Elliptical – 2 min
  • Bodyweight Glute Bridges – 15
  • Standing Side Crunches – 20 reps/side

Rest 60 sec between rounds

Pace: Controlled & sustainable

Beginner Fat Loss Sample Weekly Flow

  • MonStrength (Full Body)
  • TueLow-Impact Cardio + Core
  • WedStrength + Mobility
  • ThuRest / Recovery
  • FriLight Circuit Training
  • SatActive Recovery (Walk/Yoga)
  • SunOptional Cardio / Full Rest

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Calories: 500–700 kcal deficit/day
  • Protein: High (2–2.2g/kg) to preserve muscle during fat loss
  • Carbs: Low to moderate, focus on fiber (1–2.5g/kg)
  • Fats: Moderate (0.8–1g/kg)

Cut out sugar, fast food, excess oils.

Hydration: 3–4L/day, especially with cardio.

Lifestyle & Habit Tips

  • Sleep: Aim for 7–8 hours
  • Steps: Target 7k–10k daily
  • Track food: Use apps (like MyFitnessPal) for awareness
  • Progress tracking: Tape measurements + photos monthly
  • Support: Accountability group or coach helps long-term

Supplement Suggestions (Optional)

  • Whey or Plant Protein – fill gaps in protein intake
  • Greens supplement – if veggie intake is low
  • Electrolytes – important for hydration
  • Creatine (optional) – even during fat loss, helps preserve lean mass
  • Caffeine / green tea extract – light metabolic aid (optional)
