Moderate Risk Workout Plan (WHtR 0.5 - 0.59)

Goal

Fat burning, improving cardiovascular health, and gradually building strength.

Schedule

4–5 Days/Week (Mon–Fri)

Structure

  • Warm-Up (5–10 min): Dynamic stretches or light cardio
  • Main Workout: 3–4 exercises, 3–4 sets each
  • Optional Finisher: Short burst circuit or HIIT
  • Cooldown (5 min): Static stretching + deep breathing

Workout Levels

Beginner

Monday – Cardio + Full Body Strength

  • Brisk Walking or Light Jogging – 20 min
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3x10
  • Incline Push-Ups – 3x8
  • Dumbbell Rows (or resistance bands) – 3x10
  • Plank – 2x20 sec

Tuesday – Core + Conditioning

  • Bicycle Crunches – 3x15
  • Leg Raises – 3x12
  • Mountain Climbers – 3x20 sec
  • Russian Twists – 3x20
  • Jumping Jacks – 3x30 sec

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 30–40 min brisk walk or cycling
  • Full-body stretch and mobility exercises
  • Optional: Light yoga or foam rolling

Thursday – Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Cycling or Jogging – 20 min
  • Step-Ups (onto a low step) – 3x10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3x15
  • Seated Calf Raises – 3x15
  • Wall Sit – 2x30 sec

Friday – Full Body Circuit

  • Jump Squats – 3x12
  • Push-Up + Row (renegade row) – 3x8
  • Dumbbell Thrusters – 3x10
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps – 3x20
  • Burpees – 3x8

Intermediate

Monday – Cardio + Upper Body Strength

  • Brisk Walking or Jogging – 20 min
  • Push-Ups – 3x10
  • Resistance Band Rows – 3x12
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Plank – 3x30 sec

Tuesday – Core + Conditioning

  • Russian Twists – 3x20
  • Leg Raises – 3x12
  • Jumping Jacks – 3x30 sec
  • Bicycle Crunches – 3x20
  • Mountain Climbers – 3x30 sec

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 30–40 min brisk walk or cycling
  • Stretching or yoga flow
  • Optional: Foam rolling

Thursday – Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Brisk Walk or Jog – 20 min
  • Goblet Squats – 3x12
  • Walking Lunges – 3x10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3x15
  • Wall Sit – 2x45 sec

Friday – Full Body Power Circuit

  • Jump Squats – 3x15
  • Push-Up + Row – 3x10
  • Mountain Climbers – 3x30 sec
  • Dumbbell Thrusters – 3x12
  • Kettlebell Swings (or dumbbell) – 3x15

Advanced

Monday – Cardio + Upper Body Strength

  • Jogging or Cycling – 20–30 min
  • Push-Ups (weighted or plyometric) – 4x10
  • Pull-Ups (or assisted) – 4x6
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 4x10
  • Plank – 4x45 sec

Tuesday – Core + HIIT

  • Russian Twists (weighted) – 4x25
  • Leg Raises (weighted) – 4x15
  • Mountain Climbers – 4x30 sec
  • Plank with Reach – 3x20
  • HIIT: 30s High Knees → 30s Rest × 6 rounds

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 40–45 min brisk walk or cycling
  • Full-body mobility and deep stretching
  • Optional: Yoga or foam rolling

Thursday – Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Running (or cycling) – 20–30 min
  • Barbell Squats (or heavy goblet squats) – 4x8
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 4x8 each leg
  • Glute Bridges (weighted) – 4x12
  • Jump Lunges – 3x10 each leg

Friday – Full Body Power Circuit

  • Kettlebell Swings – 4x15
  • Burpees – 4x12
  • Dumbbell Snatch – 3x10 each side
  • AMRAP (5 min):
    • 10 Jump Squats
    • 10 Push-Ups
    • 10 Sit-Ups
    • 20 Mountain Climbers

Progression Guide

  • Increase intensity gradually: Start with lighter weights and increase over time
  • Focus on proper form to avoid injury while working on fat loss and strength
  • Add circuit training for improved metabolic conditioning
  • Switch up your cardio (e.g., switch between cycling, walking, or swimming every few weeks)
