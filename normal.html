Workout Program for Normal Weight Individuals

If you're at a normal weight and want a balanced workout program (whether for muscle gain, strength, or general fitness), here’s a well-rounded routine for you.

Goal Options:

  • Muscle Growth (Hypertrophy) – Moderate to heavy weight, 8-12 reps per set
  • Strength – Heavy weight, lower reps (4-8)
  • Fat Loss/Toning – Higher reps (12-15), more cardio

This plan will cover all aspects of fitness: strength, endurance, and flexibility.

4-Day Workout Split

  • Day 1: Upper Body (Push)
  • Day 2: Lower Body
  • Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery
  • Day 4: Upper Body (Pull)
  • Day 5: Full-Body or Cardio
  • Day 6 & 7: Rest or Light Activity

Day 1: Upper Body (Push – Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  1. Bench Press (or Push-Ups) – 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  2. Overhead Shoulder Press – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  3. Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  4. Dips (or Triceps Extensions) – 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  5. Lateral Raises – 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  6. Plank Hold – 3 x 45 sec

Day 2: Lower Body (Legs & Core)

  1. Squats (Barbell or Dumbbells) – 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  2. Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  3. Leg Press (or Bulgarian Split Squats) – 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  4. Hamstring Curls – 3 sets x 12 reps
  5. Standing Calf Raises – 3 sets x 15-20 reps
  6. Hanging Leg Raises (or Russian Twists) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery

  • Light cardio (20-30 min walk, swim, bike)
  • Mobility work or yoga

Day 4: Upper Body (Pull – Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)

  1. Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldown) – 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  2. Barbell or Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  3. Face Pulls (for rear delts & posture) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  4. Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  5. Hammer Curls – 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  6. Farmer’s Carry (Grip Strength) – 3 x 30 sec

Day 5: Full-Body (or HIIT/Cardio)

  1. Deadlifts (or Kettlebell Swings) – 4 sets x 8 reps
  2. Lunges (Bodyweight or Dumbbells) – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
  3. Push Press (Dumbbells or Barbell) – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  4. Jump Rope or Rowing Machine – 3 x 1 min
  5. Plank Variations – 3 sets

Days 6 & 7: Rest or Light Activity

  • Walking, cycling, stretching, or fun activities like sports

Extra Tips for Success:

  • Eat a Balanced Diet – Enough protein, carbs, and healthy fats
  • Progressive Overload – Gradually increase weights/reps
  • Sleep & Recovery – 7-9 hours of sleep per night
  • Hydration – At least 2-3L of water daily
