Workout Program for Overweight Individuals

If you're overweight and want a workout plan to lose fat, build strength, and improve endurance, the key is a combination of strength training + cardio + proper nutrition.

Main Goals:

  • Fat Loss – Burn calories with strength & cardio
  • Muscle Retention – Prevent muscle loss while cutting fat
  • Increased Endurance – Improve heart health & stamina
  • Sustainable & Enjoyable Routine

This 5-day plan balances weight training, cardio, and active recovery.

Weekly Workout Plan

  • Day 1: Full-Body Strength + Cardio
  • Day 2: Low-Impact Cardio + Core
  • Day 3: Upper Body Strength
  • Day 4: Lower Body Strength
  • Day 5: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) or Active Recovery
  • Day 6 & 7: Rest or Light Movement

Day 1: Full-Body Strength + Cardio

Strength Training (3 sets each, 8-12 reps):

  • Squats (Bodyweight or Dumbbells)
  • Push-ups (or Incline Push-ups)
  • Bent-over Dumbbell Rows
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press
  • Glute Bridges
  • Plank (Hold for 30-60 sec)

Cardio (20-30 min):

  • Brisk Walking / Cycling / Elliptical

Day 2: Low-Impact Cardio + Core

Cardio (30-45 min at moderate intensity):

  • Fast Walking, Swimming, Cycling, or Rowing

Core Workout (3 sets each, 12 reps):

  • Seated Knee Tucks
  • Russian Twists
  • Dead Bugs
  • Side Planks (30 sec each side)

Day 3: Upper Body Strength

  • Dumbbell Bench Press – 3 x 8-12
  • Lat Pulldown (or Assisted Pull-ups) – 3 x 8-12
  • Shoulder Lateral Raises – 3 x 12-15
  • Bicep Curls – 3 x 10-12
  • Triceps Dips (or Extensions) – 3 x 10-12

Cardio (15 min):

  • Incline Walk or Light Jog

Day 4: Lower Body Strength

  • Goblet Squats – 3 x 8-12
  • Step-Ups (Use Dumbbells if possible) – 3 x 10 each leg
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3 x 8-12
  • Calf Raises – 3 x 15-20
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3 x 12

Cardio (15 min):

  • Rowing or Cycling

Day 5: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) or Active Recovery

HIIT Circuit (4 Rounds, 30 sec each exercise, 15 sec rest):

  • Jump Squats (or Bodyweight Squats)
  • Push-Ups (or Wall Push-Ups)
  • Dumbbell Swings
  • High Knees (or Marching in Place)
  • Plank

Alternative:

  • Light Activity – Walking, Yoga, Stretching

Days 6 & 7: Rest or Active Recovery

  • Yoga, Stretching, or 30-min Walk

Bonus Tips for Fat Loss & Health:

  • Strength Training is Key – Prevents muscle loss while losing fat
  • Cardio Helps Burn Calories – Low-impact is great for joints
  • Eat a Caloric Deficit – Consume slightly fewer calories than you burn
  • Increase Protein – Helps with muscle retention (1.2-1.5g per kg body weight)
  • Hydrate – 2.5-3L of water daily
  • Sleep Well – At least 7-9 hours per night
