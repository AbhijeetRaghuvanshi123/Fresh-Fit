Lean Athletic Workout Plan (Men 6-13% / Women 16-23% BF)

Goal

Lean Gains / Muscle Definition / Performance

Target Audience

Men (6–13% BF) / Women (16–23% BF)

Training Level

Intermediate to Advanced

Caloric Intake

Slight surplus (for lean bulking) OR maintenance (for definition)

Key Focus

Progressive overload, recovery, muscular symmetry

Weekly Workout Schedule (6 Days)

Day 1 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Barbell Bench Press – 4×6–8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4×10
  • Overhead Press – 4×8
  • Lateral Raises – 4×15
  • Skull Crushers – 3×10
  • Overhead Cable Triceps Extensions – 3×12
  • Finisher: Push-up AMRAP (as many reps as possible) – 2 sets

Rest: 60–90 sec between sets

Day 2 – Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • Deadlifts – 4×5
  • Weighted Pull-ups – 4×6–10
  • Bent-over Rows – 4×8
  • Lat Pulldown (underhand grip) – 3×12
  • Barbell Curls – 3×10
  • Incline Dumbbell Curls – 3×12
  • Finisher: Face Pulls – 3×15

Rest: 90 sec between sets

Day 3 – Legs (Strength & Hypertrophy)

  • Back Squats – 4×6–8
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4×8–10
  • Walking Lunges – 3×20 steps
  • Leg Press – 3×12
  • Hamstring Curls – 3×15
  • Standing Calf Raises – 4×15–20

Rest: 60–90 sec between sets

Day 4 – Rest or Active Recovery

  • Light cardio (bike, walk, swim) – 30 min
  • Stretching / Mobility Work – 15–20 min
  • Optional yoga or foam rolling

Day 5 – Push (Strength Focus)

  • Weighted Dips – 4×6
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 4×8
  • Incline Barbell Bench – 4×6
  • Cable Chest Flyes – 3×12
  • Close-grip Push-ups – 2 sets to failure

Rest: 90 sec between sets

Day 6 – Pull (Isolation Focus)

  • Chin-ups – 4×10
  • T-Bar Row – 4×8
  • Single-arm Dumbbell Row – 3×12
  • Preacher Curls – 3×10
  • Rope Hammer Curls – 3×12
  • Shrugs – 3×15
  • Finisher: 5-min rowing machine sprint intervals

Rest: 60 sec between sets

Day 7 – Legs + Core

  • Front Squats – 4×8
  • Hip Thrusts – 3×12
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3×10 each leg
  • Seated Calf Raises – 4×20
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3×15
  • Weighted Planks – 3×45 sec hold

Rest: 60–90 sec between sets

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Calories: Maintenance or +200–300 kcal/day
  • Protein: 2–2.2g/kg body weight
  • Carbs: 3–5g/kg for energy and recovery
  • Fats: 0.8–1g/kg

Include nutrient-dense whole foods and stay hydrated.

Supplement Suggestions (Optional)

  • Creatine monohydrate (5g/day) – strength & recovery
  • Whey protein – hit daily protein goal
  • Omega-3s – anti-inflammatory, joint support
  • Electrolytes – for recovery and performance
