Healthy Zone Workout Plan (WHtR < 0.5)

Goal

Build strength, improve endurance, and tone the body.

Schedule

5 Days/Week (Mon–Fri)

Structure

  • Warm-Up (5–10 min): Dynamic stretches or light cardio
  • Main Workout: 3–4 exercises, 3–4 sets each
  • Optional Finisher: Short burst circuit or HIIT
  • Cooldown (5 min): Static stretching + deep breathing

Workout Levels

Beginner

Monday – Upper Body Strength

  • Wall Push-Ups – 3x10
  • Resistance Band Rows – 3x12
  • Seated Dumbbell Press – 3x10
  • Forearm Plank – 2x30 sec

Tuesday – Lower Body Strength

  • Bodyweight Squats – 3x10
  • Step-Ups (onto a low step) – 3x10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3x15
  • Seated Calf Raises – 3x15

Wednesday – Core + Conditioning

  • Seated Knee Lifts – 3x15
  • Dead Bugs – 3x10 each side
  • Standing Oblique Crunch – 3x10 each side
  • Marching in Place – 3x1 min

Thursday – Active Recovery

  • 20–30 min walk
  • Light stretching or yoga flow
  • Optional: Foam rolling

Friday – Full Body Circuit

  • Chair Squats – 3x12
  • Incline Push-Ups – 3x8
  • Standing Rows (bands) – 3x10
  • Step-Touch Side to Side – 3x30 sec

Intermediate

Monday – Upper Body Strength

  • Push-Ups – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3x12
  • Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps – 3x20

Tuesday – Lower Body Strength

  • Goblet Squats – 3x12
  • Walking Lunges – 3x10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3x15
  • Wall Sit – 2x30 sec

Wednesday – Core + Conditioning

  • Russian Twists – 3x20
  • Leg Raises – 3x12
  • Plank Hold – 3x45 sec
  • Jumping Jacks – 3x30 sec

Thursday – Active Recovery

  • 30 min walk or light cycling
  • Full-body mobility routine
  • Foam rolling or stretching

Friday – Full Body Circuit

  • Jump Squats – 3x12
  • Push-Up + Row – 3x10
  • Mountain Climbers – 3x30 sec
  • Dumbbell Thrusters – 3x10

Advanced

Monday – Upper Body Strength

  • Diamond Push-Ups – 4x10
  • Weighted Pull-Ups or Rows – 4x8
  • Arnold Press – 4x10
  • Plank to Push-Up – 3x12

Tuesday – Lower Body Strength

  • Barbell Squats or Heavy Goblet Squats – 4x8
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3x8 each leg
  • Hip Thrusts – 3x15
  • Jump Lunges – 3x10

Wednesday – Core + HIIT

  • Hanging Leg Raises or Weighted Crunches – 4x15
  • Plank with Reach – 3x20
  • Bicycle Crunches – 3x30
  • HIIT: 30s High Knees → 30s Rest × 6 rounds

Thursday – Active Recovery

  • 20–30 min jog, cycling, or swim
  • Full-body mobility and deep stretching
  • Optional: Sauna, ice bath, or yoga

Friday – Full Body Power Circuit

  • Kettlebell Swings – 3x15
  • Burpees – 3x12
  • Dumbbell Snatch – 3x10 each side
  • AMRAP (5 min):
    • 10 Jump Squats
    • 5 Push-Ups
    • 10 Sit-Ups
    • 20 Mountain Climbers
