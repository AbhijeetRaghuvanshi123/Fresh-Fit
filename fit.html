Fit Workout Plan (Men 14-17% / Women 24-30% BF)

Goal

Body Recomposition (Fat Loss + Muscle Gain)

Target Audience

Men (14–17% BF) / Women (24–30% BF)

Training Level

Intermediate

Caloric Intake

Near maintenance or slight deficit

Key Focus

Balanced strength, moderate cardio, muscle tone, metabolic conditioning

Weekly Workout Split (5 Days + Optional Cardio Day)

Day 1 – Full Body Strength

  • Squats – 4×6–8
  • Bench Press – 4×6
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3×10
  • Pull-ups – 3×10
  • Plank (weighted or extended) – 3×1 min

Rest: 90 sec between sets

Day 2 – HIIT + Core

  • HIIT (20 min): Sprint 30 sec / Rest 90 sec × 6–8 rounds or Rowing Intervals / Jump Rope
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3×12
  • Cable Crunches – 3×15
  • Russian Twists – 3×30 sec
  • Side Planks – 2×30 sec/side

Rest: 30–45 sec (fast-paced day)

Day 3 – Upper Body (Push Focus)

  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4×8
  • Overhead Press – 4×10
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3×15
  • Tricep Dips – 3×10
  • Cable Tricep Pushdowns – 3×12
  • Finisher: Push-up burnout – 2 sets to failure

Rest: 60 sec

Day 4 – Lower Body (Glutes, Hamstrings, Quads)

  • Deadlifts – 4×6
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3×10 per leg
  • Leg Press – 3×12
  • Hip Thrusts – 3×10
  • Standing Calf Raises – 4×15–20

Rest: 90 sec

Day 5 – Upper Body (Pull Focus)

  • Barbell Row – 4×8
  • Lat Pulldown – 3×12
  • Seated Cable Row – 3×12
  • Dumbbell Curls – 3×10
  • Preacher or Incline Curls – 3×12
  • Finisher: Rear delt flyes – 3×15

Rest: 60–90 sec

Optional Day 6 – Cardio + Core (Active Recovery / Conditioning)

  • 20–30 min steady-state cardio (jog, incline treadmill, cycling)
  • Ab wheel rollout – 3×10
  • V-ups – 3×15
  • Foam rolling & mobility

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Calories: Maintenance or ~200 kcal deficit/day
  • Protein: 2g/kg body weight
  • Carbs: Moderate (~2–3.5g/kg) based on activity
  • Fats: ~0.8–1g/kg

Prioritize whole foods, fiber, hydration, and meal timing (pre/post-workout fuel).

Supplement Suggestions (Optional)

  • Whey isolate – supports lean muscle
  • Creatine (5g/day) – improves strength & recovery
  • L-carnitine or green tea extract – mild metabolic boost (optional)
  • Magnesium or ZMA – helps recovery and sleep
