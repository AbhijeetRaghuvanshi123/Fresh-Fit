High Risk Zone Workout Plan (WHtR ≥ 0.6)

Goal

Improve overall health, reduce visceral fat, and gradually build strength and endurance.

Schedule

3–4 Days/Week (Mon–Fri)

Structure

  • Warm-Up (5–10 min): Light cardio or gentle dynamic stretches
  • Main Workout: 3–4 exercises, 2–3 sets each
  • Optional Finisher: Low-impact burst circuit
  • Cooldown (5 min): Static stretching + deep breathing

Workout Levels

Beginner

Monday – Low-Impact Cardio + Full Body Strength

  • Brisk Walking or Stationary Bike – 15–20 min
  • Chair Squats – 3x10
  • Wall Push-Ups – 3x8
  • Resistance Band Rows – 3x12
  • Seated Leg Extensions – 2x12

Tuesday – Core + Mobility

  • Standing Oblique Crunches – 3x10 each side
  • Bird Dogs – 3x10 each side
  • Seated Knee Lifts – 3x10
  • Standing Marches – 3x30 sec
  • Gentle Full-Body Stretch

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 20–30 min walk or light cycling
  • Full-body stretching and mobility exercises
  • Optional: Yoga or foam rolling

Thursday – Low-Impact Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Swimming or Water Aerobics – 20 min
  • Step-Ups (onto a low step) – 3x10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges – 3x12
  • Seated Calf Raises – 3x15
  • Wall Sit – 2x30 sec

Friday – Full Body Low-Impact Circuit

  • Chair Squats – 3x12
  • Incline Push-Ups (on a wall or bench) – 3x8
  • Dumbbell or Band Deadlifts – 3x10
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3x15
  • Marching in Place – 3x1 min

Intermediate

Monday – Low-Impact Cardio + Full Body Strength

  • Brisk Walking or Stationary Bike – 20 min
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3x12
  • Modified Push-Ups – 3x10
  • Resistance Band Rows – 3x12
  • Plank (on knees) – 3x20 sec

Tuesday – Core + Mobility

  • Leg Raises (on a mat) – 3x12
  • Standing Oblique Crunches – 3x12 each side
  • Bird Dogs – 3x12 each side
  • Side-Lying Leg Lifts – 3x10 each side
  • Gentle Full-Body Stretch

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 20–30 min walk or light cycling
  • Stretching or yoga flow
  • Optional: Foam rolling

Thursday – Low-Impact Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Cycling or Swimming – 20 min
  • Glute Bridges – 3x12
  • Step-Ups (onto a higher step) – 3x10 each leg
  • Seated Calf Raises – 3x20
  • Wall Sit – 3x30 sec

Friday – Full Body Low-Impact Circuit

  • Squats (bodyweight or chair assist) – 3x12
  • Incline Push-Ups – 3x10
  • Dumbbell or Band Rows – 3x12
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3x20
  • High Knees (low impact) – 3x1 min

Advanced

Monday – Low-Impact Cardio + Full Body Strength

  • Brisk Walking or Stationary Bike – 20–30 min
  • Bodyweight Squats (add dumbbells if able) – 4x12
  • Push-Ups (or knee push-ups) – 4x10
  • Resistance Band Rows – 4x12
  • Plank – 3x30 sec

Tuesday – Core + Low-Impact Conditioning

  • Russian Twists (without weight) – 4x15
  • Leg Raises (or with resistance band) – 4x15
  • Bird Dogs – 4x15
  • Standing Oblique Crunches – 4x12 each side
  • Low-Impact Jumping Jacks (30 sec on, 30 sec rest) – 4 rounds

Wednesday – Active Recovery

  • 30–40 min walk or light cycling
  • Full-body mobility and deep stretching
  • Optional: Yoga or foam rolling

Thursday – Low-Impact Cardio + Lower Body Strength

  • Swimming or Cycling – 20–30 min
  • Glute Bridges (add weight if possible) – 4x15
  • Step-Ups (with added weight or higher step) – 4x12 each leg
  • Seated Calf Raises – 4x20
  • Wall Sit – 3x45 sec

Friday – Full Body Low-Impact Circuit

  • Kettlebell Swings (or dumbbell) – 4x15
  • Burpees (modified or low-impact) – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Snatch (light weight) – 3x10 each side
  • Squat to Press (light dumbbells) – 4x12
  • Marching High Knees – 3x1 min

Progression Guide

  • Low-Impact Focus: Prioritize activities like swimming, cycling, and walking to reduce joint stress.
  • Increase Slowly: Start with bodyweight exercises and gradually increase weight or resistance.
  • Consistency is Key: Focus on regular movement, and avoid overexertion. Aim for steady progress, not intensity.
  • Monitor Health: Make sure the user listens to their body and adjusts intensity if necessary.
